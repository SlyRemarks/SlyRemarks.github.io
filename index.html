Emoncms.org Pricing

Emoncms.org is pay-as-you-go

Buy Emoncms.org credits from the
OpenEneryMonitor Shop

Buy any hardware from the OpenEneryMonitor Shop
and you will get Emoncms.org credits worth
20% of the items in your cart

1 × Emoncms.org credit = £1 (GBP)

1 × Emoncms.org credit = 1 × data feed per year
